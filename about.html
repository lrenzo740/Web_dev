The Jaguar Drum and Bugle Corps from the University of San Jose-Recoletos is a premier ensemble driven by a passion for musicality, discipline, and excellence. With every performance, we strive to inspire and captivate, showcasing our dedication to the art of music and the precision of drill. Rooted in tradition yet always innovating, we aim to foster growth, unity, and resilience among our members, upholding the values of our university while pursuing the highest standards of performance. Join us as we transform passion into unforgettable experiences, on and off the field.
